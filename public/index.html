BLKLST Records – Underground record label from Helsinki, Finland with attitude

BLKLST Records is an underground record label from Helsinki, Finland with attitude. BLKLST Records is an independent underground music label releasing dark techno and experimental electronic music. Artists include 3BOLA, EBU, General FVKUP, Sinister Strike and SXCXDXL.
